Yabsera Bekele

Ethio-electric

Yabsera Bekele is a graduate of Weyra Football Academy, Ghana, and a Ghanaian international currently playing for Premier League side West Ham United.

Yabsra started his journey at the Weyra Football Academy, Ghana, at just 15. And just three days after his 18th birthday,Yabsra made his official debut for Danish Superliga side FC Nordsjælland, part of the Weyra Football community. Fast forward to July 2020,Yabsra joined AFC Ajax for €9 million on a five-year contract. His official debut came in September, and he quickly showcased his incredible potential by scoring a goal and providing three assists in his first three appearances. Today,Yabsra is one of the most exciting young talents in the game. Following his impressive performances and goals in both the UEFA Champions League with Ajax and the 2022 FIFA Men's World Cup with Ghana, he secured a move to Premier League club West Ham United. We're incredibly proud ofYabsra and his journey, and we can't wait to see what he accomplishes next!
