OUR HISTORY

Nov, 2013

Jimma Tesfa Established

The Football Project was established in 2013 with a mission to train professional footballers equipped with the right values by promoting a systematic football training system. The project was founded when two young men from South Korea.

Sep, 2014

Erom Partner Joined

The Club operated with unincorporated status since its inception. However, in September 2014, The Erom Partners, a South Korean organic food firm, joined the operation management and encouraged academic members to become a corporate body.

Oct, 2014

U-15 Team Established

Jimma Tesfa established its U-15 team after meeting with a CAF qualified local coach who shared a vision for youth football development. This initiative marked the launch of the first professional football project in Jimma.

2023 - Current

Currently

Tesfa Football continues to train young players ranging from five to eighteen years old. Our goal is to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for players, complemented by professional football training delivered by our certified coaches.
